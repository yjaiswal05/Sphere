What does this mean?

MX Records: Mail Exchange records specify which mail servers are responsible for receiving email messages on behalf of your domain.

SPF Records: Sender Policy Framework is a DNS TXT record that specifies which mail servers are authorized to send emails on behalf of your domain.

DMARC Records: Domain-based Message Authentication, Reporting, and Conformance provides domain owners with more control over how their emails are authenticated and how unauthenticated emails should be treated.
